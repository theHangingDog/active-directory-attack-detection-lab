SMB Enumeration


title: SMB Enumeration Scan Correlation
id: 2f9b6c8d-8a4e-4c7b-9d1a-7e2c3b9f4a6d
description: Detects multiple SMB connections to more than 10 unique hosts within 1 minute.
status: experimental
author: Arkaprava
date: 2026/02/07
correlation:
  type: threshold
  rules:
    - 7f3a9a2b-4d1a-4c9c-bf1a-3c2d9b8e4f1a   # reference to the base rule below
  group-by:
    - src_ip
  timeframe: 1m
  condition:
    count(dst_ip) > 10
    

title: Suspicious SMB Enumeration Scan
id: 7f3a9a2b-4d1a-4c9c-bf1a-3c2d9b8e4f1a
description: Detects SMB enumeration scans across multiple hosts (port 445/139), often used in reconnaissance prior to lateral movement.
status: experimental
author: Arkaprava
date: 2026/02/07
references:
  - https://attack.mitre.org/techniques/T1046/
logsource:
  category: network_connection
detection:
  selection:
    dst_port:
      - 445
      - 139
  condition: selection and count(dst_ip) > 10
fields:
  - src_ip
  - dst_ip
  - dst_port
falsepositives:
  - Legitimate vulnerability scans
  - IT asset discovery tools
level: medium
tags:
  - attack.discovery
  - attack.t1046

title: Unauthorized SMB Share Access
id: 9a2d6c4e-5b7f-4f8a-9d2b-6e1f3c7a8b9d
description: Detects attempts to access SMB shares with null or guest authentication, or access to non-default shares that may contain sensitive data.
status: experimental
author: Arkaprava
date: 2026/02/07
references:
  - https://attack.mitre.org/techniques/T1077/
logsource:
  category: network
detection:
  selection_auth:
    auth_type:
      - null
      - guest
  selection_share:
    share_name|contains:
      - IPC$
      - backup
      - temp
  condition: selection_auth or selection_share
fields:
  - src_ip
  - username
  - share_name
falsepositives:
  - Legacy systems with guest access enabled
  - Misconfigured test environments
level: high
tags:
  - attack.lateral_movement
  - attack.t1077
